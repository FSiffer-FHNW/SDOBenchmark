There exist several active forecasts for solar activity. A nonextensive list of examples are:

  • The 24h prediction of solar monitor at solarmonitor.org
  • The manual forecasts of the NOAA Space Weather Prediction Center (e.g. here or )
  • A RandomForest applied on HMI data, Arxiv

Besides the mentioned predictions, plenty of research about solar activities are being conducted.
To our knowledge, noone has applied CNNs on AIA and HMI image products for flare prediction.
