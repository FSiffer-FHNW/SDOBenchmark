SDOBenchmark is a machine learning image dataset for the prediction of solar flares.

Solar flares are intense bursts of radiation which can disrupt the power grids of a continent, shut down the GPS system or irradiate people in space with life-threatening X-rays.
Developing systems for predicting solar flares would allow us to precisely aim our research sensors at upcoming events, and eventually enable countermeasures against such worst case scenarios.
