SDOBenchmark is a machine learning image dataset for the prediction of solar flares.

Solar flare eruptions can reach scales whose emissions blow out the electric grids of a continent, shut down the GPS system or contaminate people in space with life-threatening X-rays.
Developing predictions for solar flares would allow us to precisely align our research sensors for all upcoming events, and eventually enable countermeasures against such worst case scenarios.
